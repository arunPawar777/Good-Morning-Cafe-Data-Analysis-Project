# Good-Morning-Cafe-Data-Analysis-Project
This project presents a detailed analysis of sales data for Good Morning Cafe. The goal is to extract meaningful insights from raw data and help understand business performance, customer behavior, and product trends.

Objectives:
Analyze daily and monthly sales performance
Identify top-selling products
Understand customer purchase patterns
Track revenue growth and trends
Provide data-driven insights for decision making

The dataset contains:
Order details
Product names
Quantity sold
Revenue
Date of transactions

Tools & Technologies:
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Data Cleaning
Charts & Visualization

Key Insights:
Identified highest revenue-generating products
Found peak sales time periods
Analyzed customer preferences
Observed trends in sales growth
